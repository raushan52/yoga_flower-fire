Welcome Home

Our rooms and traditional Lumbung style bungalows offer a place to stay surrounded by nature and organic materials without compromising on comfort. All our accommodation options come with unlimited yoga, breakfast and lunch as part of our inclusive yoga stay packages.

Yoga Stay Highlights

We offer a number of facilities to make your stay a truly memorable experience.

Internet

WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge

Relax area

Our two chill out zones offer indoor and outdoor seating with a plunge pool to lounge around in

Library

You can leave your heavy books at home. We offer a small library here with holiday reads and inspirational literature

Food & Drink

With delicious yet wholesome home-made breakfasts and lunches included from our café, you will nourish both taste buds and body

With yoga, breakfast and lunch already included in our prices, all you need to do is choose your room type.

We provide four types of accommodation to suit the varying needs and budgets of our guests: Deluxe Lumbung bungalows, Deluxe Family Bungalow, Queen rooms and a Share/Family room.

Each room comes with an en-suite bathroom, hot water, A/C, fan, bedside lights, 100% Egyptian cotton sheets and microfiber pillow and duvet, wardrobe storage, workstation (bungalows only), natural toiletries, mini fridge, tea and coffee making facilities and display a high attention to detail in the linen, toiletries, lighting and design lay out.

Bungalows

Deluxe King (Lumbung) Bungalows with Garden View
(2 bungalows / 2 people per bungalow)Our 36 m² Deluxe King Bungalow has a veranda, garden views, semi-outdoor bathroom and a king size bed or two singles. Please email us at flowersandfireyoga@gmail.com for more information about rates and availability.

Large Deluxe King (Lumbung) Bungalow with Loft / Family Bungalow
(1 bungalow / 3 or 4 people per bungalow)Our 45 m² Large Deluxe King Bungalow is located next to our Mango Yoga Studio, and has a veranda, semi-outdoor bathroom and built-in loft. The lower area can be configured depending on your needs into a king size or two singles.
The loft queen size bed would require bed sharing for double occupancy. Please email us at flowersandfireyoga@gmail.com for more information about rates and availability.

Rates & Availability

Share/Family Room

Deluxe Share/Family Room
(1 room / 4 people per room)Our 34 m² Share/Dormitory has an en-suite bathroom and offers a comfortable option for yogi’s and travellers who like to share and are on a budget.
With the same luxuries as our private rooms, this room has A/C and ceiling fans and each single bed has 100% Egyptian cotton sheets, micro-fiber pillow and duvet, a mosquito net and individual power plugs and USB ports.
There is also the option to book the entire room (either four single beds, two single beds & one king bed or two king beds). Please email us at flowersandfireyoga@gmail.com for more information about rates and availability.

Rates & Availability

Queen Rooms

Deluxe Queen Rooms
(2 room / 2 people per room)Our 20 m² Deluxe Queen Room offers a cosy and comfortable stay with mosquito nets and an en-suite indoor bathroom. The queen bed cannot be converted to 2 x twin beds so double occupancy in this room requires bed sharing. Please note that these room types are located just behind our café so they can get a little café noise and buzz during the day. All guests are supplied with high-quality earplugs though to reduce the impact of this noise. Please email us at flowersandfireyoga@gmail.com for more information about rates and availability.

Venue Hire

Looking for the perfect space for your next retreat, training, conference or event? Gili Air’s lack of motorized vehicles, easy barefoot lifestyle, stunning warm waters and snorkeling/dive sites, provides a wonderful backdrop for yoga, personal development, corporate or creative trainings and retreats. Offering competitive rental rates and attention to detail in facilities and services, Flowers and Fire Yoga Garden Gili Air is an intimate and perfect venue to host you and your guests. Our retreat packages all include daily breakfast buffet, refill drinking water, daily fresh fruit and tea, yoga mats and props, access to our audio/visual display facilities, use of the pool, studio usage. We also have a range of different catering packages. Take advantage of our retreat consultation service informed by years of experience running and facilitating retreats on and off the island. We have a good sense of what works well in this setting; let us guide you through the Gilis. With an abundance of additional local activities available to enrich your program, we would love to help you design a magical retreat, colored in with all the wonderful things the island and region have to offer. Please email us at flowersandfireyoga@gmail.com if you would like us to send you our retreat brochure and pricing information.
