Yoga Paradise

We are a new yoga studio and creative community space situated in the centre of the Indonesian paradise island of Gili Air. With an emphasis on high-quality service, heartfelt teaching, sustainability and inclusive community, we aim to create a magical experience for all our guests – whether they practice, eat, co-work, or stay with us.

We are excited and grateful for the opportunity to continue offering sessions to you during these surreal and unusual times.

For these online offerings I hope to share some of the practices and contemplations that have illuminated my path and that of our teaching team. Yoga Classes in the Centre

Our yoga classes at Flowers & Fire cater to a variety of styles and levels of experience – from first-timers to experienced yogis and yoginis. Our aim is to create a soulful experience to dissolve the pressure of physical and mental stressors, facilitating your journey towards expansiveness and ease. We offer daily drop-in yoga classes in our treehouse or mango garden studio as well as workshops and retreats. Check out our timetable for more information.

Yoga Stay Packages

With an emphasis on high-quality, heartfelt teaching, service and facilities, Flowers & Fire Yoga Garden aims to create a magical experience for all guests. Teachers are known for their soulful, creative yoga offerings and classes are open to all levels of experience. Included in your stay package is unlimited yoga, with classes ranging from foundations for beginners, chakra- activating vinyasa flow classes, yin, slow flow, restorative classes, pranayama (breath work) and meditation. Our yoga stay offering is designed to help you to nourish and revitalise yourself, inside and out.

Wholefoods Cafe

Our vegetarian wholefoods cafe serves natural and delicious wholefood creations with plenty of fresh, minimally processed and enzym- rich ingredients. Our aim is to make virtuous food taste wicked – nurturing your body, soul and taste buds!

The Island

With turquoise waters and stunning reefs, perfect views for sunsets and sunrises, and laidback vibes, Gili Air is lovingly referred to as the paradise island. With no motorised vehicles to disturb the peace and the beachfront only a 20-minute walk in any direction, Gili Air lives up to that name with ease.

Workshops & Events

Every week we compliment our class schedule by hosting different workshops, trainings and community events such as our Movie Night, Yoga for Beginners, or our events and workshop series with offerings based on the unique talents, passions and interests of our current teachers and special guests.
