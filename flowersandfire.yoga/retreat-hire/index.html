Flowers & Fire Yoga Yoga Garden – Gili Air

Retreat Hire

Flowers and Fire is more than just a retreat space. It’s more than another island getaway.

It’s a healing haven that welcomes guests to explore, rest, unwind, create connections, and relax into the softness of being.

Situated in the centre of the Indonesian paradise island Gili Air, Flowers and Fire Yoga Garden blends traditional Javanese artisanal teak details with local bamboo and ulang ulang style structures. This has created an intimate and magical space which maximizes interaction with the beautiful surrounding environment.

Our two yoga studios, cafe and accommodations open out onto our gardens allowing a seamless indoor/outdoor experience. This reflects our site specific design principles. These principles have meant that we preserved existing trees and created our buildings around them.

Our magnificent mango tree holds space at the centre of everything.

Retreat Hire Highlights

We offer a number of facilities to make your retreat stay a truly memorable for you and your guests.

Breakfast & Snacks

With delicious yet wholesome home-made buffet breakfasts included from our café, you will nourish both taste buds and body. Drinking water, tea, coffee and daily fresh fruit will also be served.

Internet Access & Library

WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.You can leave your heavy books at home. We offer a small library here with holiday reads and inspirational literature

Relax Area

Our two chill out zones offer indoor and outdoor seating with a plunge pool to lounge around in. Our centre is 5-7 minute walk to sunrise and sunset beachside locations.

Yoga Studio

For groups of 12 students or less this includes unlimited use of the Mango Garden Studio and limited use of the Large Tree Top View Studio. For larger groups this can be negotiated further.

Studio Use

Situated on the second floor of the main building our L shaped Open Air Yoga Studio is 187 square meters and provides you with 360 degree village and tree top views.
There is also a bathroom with a toilet and a shower, tea and coffee making facilities, a bamboo diamond shaped banister running all the way around and built in niches with yoga mats and props.

We have cushions, bolsters, high quality Jute yoga mats, cork blocks, straps, and some pilates gear such as thera-bands, 1kg hand weights and small inflatable balls.

You can play music and present audio visual displays on our sound system, projector and large screen and temperature/lighting/privacy can be managed with built in fans and bamboo blinds. We also have silent disco headset technology, perfect for ecstatic dance sessions or evening movie nights.

Our 50 square meter Mango Studio is also open air and situated in our gardens looking onto our majestic mango tree. Curtained by mosquito nets and installed with 4 wall fans you can enjoy the garden view and fresh air without the mosquitoes enjoying you!!!! When it isn’t being used as a studio, this area is styled with days beds, cushions and rugs and makes a great meditation, informal discussion or art space.

For groups of 12 students or less our pricing includes unlimited use of the Mango Garden Studio and limited use of the Large Tree Top View Studio. For larger groups, we can negotiate greater studio usage of the Large Tree Top View on a case by case basis depending on your needs and requirementsand seasonal factors.

Accommodation

We provide three levels of accommodation to suit the varying budgets of your retreat guests; 4 bed cave, Queen rooms and traditional Lumbung deluxe bungalows.

Our rooms and traditional Lumbung style bungalows offer a place to stay surrounded by nature and organic materials without compromising on comfort. Each room comes with an en-suite bathroom, hot water, air-conditioning, fan, bedside lights, 100% Egyptian cotton sheets and microfiber pillow and duvet, towels, wardrobe storage, workstation (bungalows only), natural toiletries, mini fridge, tea and coffee making facilities and display a high attention to detail in the linen, toiletries, lighting and design lay out. We provide four types of accommodation to suit the varying needs and budgets of your retreat guests: deluxe lumbung bungalows, deluxe family bungalow, queen rooms and a share/family room. Our maximum capacity of 16 guests requires that every room be shared-including the rooms with queen beds that cannot be separated.

If your accommodation needs exceed our capacity, we can help you arrange alternative accommodation with some of our partners. For those participants staying off site, we will charge an access fee per person per day which includes breakfast, free refill drinking water, daily fresh fruit and tea, yoga mats and props, use of pool and studio usage.

Vegan & Vegetarian Wholefoods Cafe

Our vegetarian wholefoods Flowers & Fire Café serves natural and delicious wholefood creations with plenty of fresh, minimally processed and enzyme-rich ingredients. You have the option to customise, too, with your favourite fruit, veggies and superfood top-offs.

Our café menu is available onsite throughout the day until 7pm.
